i let you fall

Song: Nature Sound:

Description:

looking at this scene, i honestly felt like the ladder. the only support holding up the person repairing the house. if the ladder moves, it all collapses, no progress is made, the person is hurt. in truth, i was indeed the ladder and i did indeed move. stagnating progress, hurting the climber.

hold on

Song: Nature Sound:

Description:

The bag is anyone who needs to hold on. the tree is anyone willing to help them hold on. in this sense, I felt like the tree offering support for the bag. not necessarily choosing to do so, but helping none the less. ultimately it is the wind, or the strength of the plastic that maintains its grip on the tree. without it, the bag floats into the unknown, drowns in the sea of air and land. am i the bag? the tree? the wind?
